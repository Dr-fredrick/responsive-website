Our Story

Heavenly Gospel began as a vision to spread the light of Christ to every corner of the world. Founded with passion for outreach, healing, and spiritual growth, our ministry now reaches individuals and families through prayer, teaching, worship, and community support.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ with power and compassion, raise spiritually strong believers, and impact communities with the love of God through outreach, prayer, and discipleship.

Our Vision

To be a global ministry that brings salvation, healing, and transformation to lives through the Word of God, worship, and acts of compassion.

Our Core Beliefs

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God.
  • Jesus Christ is the only way to salvation.
  • The Holy Spirit empowers believers for life and ministry.
  • Prayer, worship, and evangelism are the heartbeat of our faith.
  • We are called to serve the community and love our neighbors.
