Our Ministries

Evangelism & Outreach

We preach the Gospel to the lost and unreached through street missions, crusades, and digital platforms.

Healing & Deliverance

Through prayer and the power of the Holy Spirit, we minister healing, restoration, and spiritual freedom.

Teaching & Discipleship

We raise mature believers through Bible studies, mentorship, and discipleship programs.

Worship & Prayer

We cultivate a life of intimate worship and intercession through regular services, vigils, and worship nights.

Community Compassion

We serve the community with acts of love, including food drives, school outreach, and orphan support.
